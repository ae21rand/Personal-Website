STUDENT, DESIGNER, PHOTOGRAPHER

Student, Designer & Photographer with a Passion for UX

Hi, I'm Lexi! I'm a computer science student at Siena college studying cybersecurity with a multimedia design minor. This website showcases my artwork, photography, and design projects. Design is a big passion of mine, and I hope to incorporate it into my career. It drives me to create work that is clean, intentional, and thoughtfully crafted.

My multimedia design minor has helped me refine my skills and explore new creative areas, making me a more versatile designer. Above all, I strive to create intuitive, beautiful, and functional designs that can be used and enjoyed by anyone.


Siena Beverage Institute

In the summer of 2024, I joined the Siena Beverage Institute, an organization at my college where I had the opportunity to deepen my understanding of the history and tasting profiles of various alcoholic beverages.

Through this experience, I also explored photography, design, and content creation, applying the skills I learned in class to real-world projects and gaining valuable hands-on experience outside the classroom.

I'm excited to continue my work with the Beverage Institute throughout my time at Siena!
